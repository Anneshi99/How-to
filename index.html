Chicken Fried Rice Recipes

It's quick, easy, and delicious



Prep: 10mins

Cook: 10mins

Total: 20mins

Servings: 2

Ingredients

  • 1 Bowl of rice
  • 2 Eggs
  • Garlic
  • Salt
  • Scallions
  • Onions
  • Chicken
  • Peas
  • Carrot
  • Soy sauce
  • Sesame oil
  • Msg
  • Salt and pepper

Directions

  1. Mince the garlic and dice the scallions, onion, chicken, and carrot.
  2. Beat two egg and add a large pinch salt.
  3. Add some oil into the pan and when it hot enough pour in the egg and scramble it. When it 3/4cooked take it out and put it into a plate
  4. Add a bit more oil in to the pan and add the garlic and onion then the chicken and stir fry it.
  5. When the chicken is half way cook add the carrot and peas and keep stir frying.
  6. When everything is almost cook through add the bowl of rice and the scramble egg.
  7. Pour in soy sauce and cook, stirring until heated through, and then add the sesame oil.
  8. When it's about done add in the scallion and stir it with seasonings like MSG or salt and pepper to your liking.

Tips

  • Use overnight rice because it's more firm and better when use to stir fry.
  • A pinch of salt to the egg give the egg more flavor.
  • Add the sesame oil near the end, if you add too early the flavor will disappear.
  • Remember to toss and stir throughout cooking so the flavor and temperature remains balanced.
